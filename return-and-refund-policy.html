Return & Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction.

1. Introduction

At DARKIS, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we are here to help. This Return & Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for returning products and requesting refunds.

2. Eligibility for Returns

You may return a product within 7 days of delivery if the following conditions are met:

  • The product is unused, unworn, and in its original condition
  • All original tags, packaging, and accessories are included
  • The product is not marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • You have proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or personalized orders
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Gift cards
  • Products that have been used or damaged by customer

4. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us at khumo@darkis.co.za within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for our return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item back using the provided shipping label

5. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless:

  • The product received is defective or damaged
  • We sent the wrong product
  • The return is due to our error

In these cases, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label.

6. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed.

7. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for different colors of the same product. To request an exchange:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Specify the product you want to exchange and the replacement
  • Exchanges are subject to product availability

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please:

  • Contact us immediately upon receipt
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will arrange for replacement or full refund at no extra cost

9. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 7 business days after processing, please:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company (may take time to post)
  • Contact us if the issue persists

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Return & Refund Policy, please contact us at:

Email: khumo@darkis.co.za
